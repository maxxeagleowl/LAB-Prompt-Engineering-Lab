In this lab, I tested and improved prompts for sentiment analysis, product description generation, and data extraction. The initial zero-shot prompts produced valid outputs but showed strong inconsistency across multiple runs, especially for product descriptions with only 13.33% consistency. By adding clear instructions, output constraints, and structure, the prompts became significantly more stable. Few-shot prompting worked best for sentiment classification and led to 100% consistency. For product descriptions, adding structure and reducing unnecessary constraints improved consistency to 86.67%. Data extraction was already consistent in content, but introducing a fixed JSON schema made the output production-ready. A key learning was that both missing constraints and over-constraining prompts can cause issues, and prompts must be tested across multiple runs to ensure reliability.
